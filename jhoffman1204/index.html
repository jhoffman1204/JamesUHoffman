Outdoor Benches Project

For my Eagle Scout project, I contacted my local Ossining Children's Center to ask if they needed any major projects done. They said that they needed new benches and tables for their outdoors area so I met with a carpenter and we discussed what the best design would be. I sketched out the designs and gathered all the materials, then led a workshop on safety and we built the benches in my backyard.
